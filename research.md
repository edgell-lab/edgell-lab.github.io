<div class="research-section">
  <h2>CRISPR-Based Gene Editing</h2>
  <p>
    We use evolutionary, genetic, structural and biochemical approaches to design, test,
    and optimize CRISPR-based nucleases for precise and efficient gene editing. Our work
    focuses on engineering and improving the specificity, efficiency, and delivery of
    CRISPR tools for a range of applications.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="research-section">
  <h2>Algae Biotechnology</h2>
  <p>
    We develop genome editing tools for eukaryotic microalgae, including
    <em>Chlamydomonas reinhardtii</em>. Algae offer a sustainable platform for
    producing proteins, biofuels, and therapeutics. We have demonstrated the
    production of SARS-CoV-2 diagnostic proteins in algae, and continue to
    expand our algae engineering toolkit.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="research-section">
  <h2>Microbial Population Control & Synthetic Biology</h2>
  <p>
    We engineer genetic systems to control microbial populations, including
    biocontainment strategies based on genetic entanglement. These approaches
    have broad applications in biosafety, industrial biotechnology, and
    the study of microbial ecosystems.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="research-section">
  <h2>Treatment of Human Disease</h2>
  <p>
    We apply our gene editing tools toward the treatment of human genetic disease,
    developing precision approaches to correct pathogenic mutations with
    high specificity and minimal off-target effects.
  </p>
</div>
